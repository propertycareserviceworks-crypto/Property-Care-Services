Our Services

We offer a variety of packages to meet your needs:

❄️ Snow Removal (Basic, Premium, VIP)

Service Details

First wave clearing during heavy snowfalls: early morning (before 7 AM) or afternoon, depending on when the snow occurs, to keep walkways accessible during the day.

Basic
• Snow cleared within 24 hours after snowfall ends
• Second clearing for snow > 2.5 cm (1 in)
• Up to 1 visit per 24 hours during heavy or ongoing snowfall
• Ideal for clients seeking reliable, cost-effective service

Premium
• Snow cleared within 12 hours after snowfall ends
• First wave clearing during heavy snowfalls (before 7 AM or afternoon)
• Second clearing same day for snow > 2.5 cm (1 in)
• Up to 2 visits per 24 hours during active snowfall
• Designed for customers who want faster access and added convenience

VIP
• Snow cleared within 6 hours after snowfall ends
• Priority first wave clearing (early morning or as soon as snow stops)
• Second clearing same day for snow > 2.5 cm (1 in), plus an extra visit if snowfall exceeds 5 cm (2 in)
• Up to 3 visits per 24 hours during heavy snowfall
• Perfect for clients seeking the fastest response and peace of mind
